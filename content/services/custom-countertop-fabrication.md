Some projects don't fit a standard slab-and-install job — waterfall edges on an island, an outdoor kitchen that needs weather-rated material, or an odd-shaped layout that needs custom templating. This is where in-house fabrication makes the difference.

## What's included

- On-site templating for irregular or multi-level layouts
- Waterfall edge and mitered corner fabrication
- Outdoor-rated material selection for lanais and summer kitchens
- Integrated sinks, drainboards, and custom cutouts
- Coordination with cabinetry and plumbing trades on your project

## Why homeowners choose us

Custom work is where fabrication experience actually matters. We've templated and cut enough non-standard layouts to know where seams should fall on a waterfall edge and how outdoor material needs to be sealed differently than an indoor kitchen — details that get missed by shops that only handle straightforward runs.
