Luxury vinyl plank has become the go-to flooring choice for Florida homes, and for good reason. It's completely waterproof, stands up to pets and kids, and now comes in finishes that are nearly impossible to distinguish from real hardwood or natural stone.

## What's included

- Product selection guidance based on your budget, traffic, and style
- Subfloor inspection and prep for a flat, stable base
- Click-lock or glue-down installation, whichever suits the space
- Trim, transitions, and baseboard work for a finished look
- Waterproof-rated options for kitchens, bathrooms, and laundry rooms

## Why homeowners choose us

LVP is forgiving material, but a bad installation still shows. We square every room before we start, plan plank layout so seams and cuts fall where they won't be noticed, and make sure transitions between rooms are tight and level. It's the detail work that separates a floor that looks good on day one from one that still looks good five years later.
