No two granite slabs are the same, which is exactly why homeowners choose it — a granite countertop is a natural, one-of-a-kind surface that no other material can replicate. It's heat-resistant, durable, and ages well with proper sealing.

## What's included

- Slab selection at the yard so you see the exact stone going into your home
- Precise templating and layout to showcase the best veining
- Fabrication with your choice of edge profile
- Sealing at installation for stain resistance
- Sink, cooktop, and outlet cutouts finished cleanly

## Why homeowners choose us

We walk clients through slab selection in person whenever possible, because granite is bought by the piece, not the sample chip. What you see at the yard is what ends up in your kitchen — no surprises in veining or color once it's installed.
