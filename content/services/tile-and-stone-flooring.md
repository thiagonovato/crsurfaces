Tile is the standard for wet areas and high-traffic rooms across Florida homes, and the difference between a mediocre tile job and a great one comes down to layout planning, substrate prep, and grout work. CR Surfaces handles all three.

## What's included

- Layout planning to center tile, minimize slivers, and align patterns
- Cement board or uncoupling membrane installation over subfloor
- Precision cutting around fixtures, corners, and transitions
- Grouting, sealing, and caulking for a watertight finish
- Large-format porcelain, natural stone, and mosaic work

## Why homeowners choose us

Tile forgives almost nothing — an uneven substrate or rushed layout shows up permanently in the grout lines. We slow down at the planning stage so the installation itself goes fast and looks intentional, not improvised.
