Hardwood floors are one of the few upgrades that pay for themselves in resale value while giving your home warmth and character no other material matches. CR Surfaces installs both solid and engineered hardwood throughout Sarasota and Tampa, and we refinish existing floors that have lost their shine to scratches, sun fade, or years of foot traffic.

## What's included

- On-site consultation and moisture testing before any material is ordered
- Subfloor prep, leveling, and moisture barrier installation where needed
- Precision installation with attention to expansion gaps and transitions
- Sanding, staining, and multi-coat finishing for refinishing projects
- Final walkthrough and care instructions for your specific wood species

## Why homeowners choose us

Florida's humidity is unforgiving on wood flooring if it isn't installed correctly. We acclimate every batch of material to your home's conditions and use installation methods suited to our climate, so your floors stay flat and tight for decades instead of cupping or gapping after the first humid summer.
